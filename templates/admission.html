Admissions Requirements

What you'll need to complete your application

General Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Application fee ($75 non-refundable)
  • Birth certificate copy
  • Immunization records
  • Recent physical examination

Academic Records

  • Official transcripts (grades K-12)
  • Standardized test results (if available)
  • Current year report card
  • Teacher recommendations (forms provided)

Additional Materials

  • Student essay (grades 6-12)
  • Parent statement
  • Interview (scheduled after application)
  • Entrance assessment (scheduled separately)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

We have rolling admissions but recommend applying by January 15th for the following academic year to ensure consideration for financial aid and to secure your spot. Late applications are accepted based on availability.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for middle and high school students, as well as need-based financial aid for qualifying families. Approximately 35% of our students receive some form of financial assistance.

Can we schedule a campus tour?

Absolutely! We encourage all prospective families to visit our campus. Tours are available Monday through Friday by appointment. We also host monthly open houses where you can meet teachers and current parents.

What is your student-teacher ratio?

Our average student-teacher ratio is 15:1, with most classes having between 12-18 students. This allows for personalized attention while still maintaining a vibrant classroom dynamic.
